Onche your administrator have granted your access you will receive an email of authorization.

Request Access

Onche you have received the "Access Authorization" by your admin follow the instructions and assign your password and enjoy the tool!

Creating Parameters

Once you have displayed the menu look for the servers section and click on the "Parameters" section.

Add New Profile

Once you have accessed go to the right section at bottom of the page and look for the button with the character "+" and click on it to create a new parameter template.

Creating New Parameter

In order to create a new "Parameter" we need t oselect a Category Group, assign Name, brief Description of the parameter and click the save button.
We can add as many parameters as needed for our execution.
Take into cosideration that the parameters need to be assigned to a "Category Group" and they can only be used under that category
